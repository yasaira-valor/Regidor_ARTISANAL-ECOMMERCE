ABOUT US

Our mission is to empower Filipino artisan families to overcome poverty by reimagining time-honored local craft for the global marketplace.

As a social enterprise, we commit to reinvesting a majority of our profits to achieve this mission.

OUR GUIDING PRINCIPLES

DESIGN

We blend contemporary design and traditional techniques to create a unique aesthetic of understated elegance.

Together with our partners and master artisans, we continually explore, experiment, and innovate with natural materials to come up with original, relevant, and transformative creations.

TRADITION

We celebrate centuries-old craftsmanship passed on through generations.

Each product is handmade by master artisans in their local communities, a unique piece of art reflecting the essence of their craft.

INCLUSION

We envision a world undivided. We foster collaboration by inviting local communities along on the creative journey.

We believe art is for all - we thus strive to sell our products at reasonable prices while ensuring the artisans and communities that created them get a fair share.
